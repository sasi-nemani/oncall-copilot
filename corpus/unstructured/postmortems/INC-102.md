# Postmortem — INC-102: login latency climbed after auth-a55

- **Service:** auth
- **Severity:** SEV2
- **Duration:** 2026-06-25T14:03:00Z → 2026-06-25T15:20:00Z

## What happened
Login latency climbed after auth-a55.

## Root cause
The root cause was auth-a55 added a synchronous call to a slow LDAP endpoint.

## Resolution
We feature-flagged the LDAP lookup off, and latency_p95 returned to normal.
