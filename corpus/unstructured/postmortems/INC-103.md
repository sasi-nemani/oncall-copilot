# Postmortem — INC-103: payments failures from an upstream processor outage

- **Service:** payments
- **Severity:** SEV1
- **Duration:** 2026-06-26T02:40:00Z → 2026-06-26T03:35:00Z

## What happened
Payments failures from an upstream processor outage.

## Root cause
The root cause was the upstream card processor returned 503s during their maintenance window.

## Resolution
We failed over to the secondary processor, and error_rate returned to normal.

## Follow-ups
- Add an automated check that ties a firing alert to any deploy in the preceding 30 minutes.
